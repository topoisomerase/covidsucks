What We Do

Solidarity is a student-run website that connects Friends in need of resources with Friends that have them during the COVID-19 pandemic. We guide you through each step so you can easily pick up food, toiletries, and medical supplies, or have them dropped off at your doorstep.

How It Works

If you are in need of supplies, you can search for what people are already offering in your area, or if you can't find what you need, you can post a request. Once your request is accepted, you will coordinate a pick-up or drop-off time and you'll have your goods!

If you want to donate supplies, you can search for what people are requesting in your area or post what you are offering.

Once you receive a request, you will coordinate a pick-up or drop-off time with the person you're helping and make their day!

We want to limit physical interactions between requesters and helpers as much as possible, so please be careful when handling items. Wash your hands, use gloves, and disinfect before and after retrieving and dropping so you can off resources.
